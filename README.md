# Cardano-governance-proposal-summaries

Each week during the Gimbal Labs Open Spaces Thursdays 14 30 UTC the community gathers and we make about 15 minutes to review current D Rep propos and exercise governance. One person volunteers to gather some information and presented and leaded discussion. Documents created are kept in this repo.
